Where are we?
우리는 어디에 있어요?
ulineun eodie iss-eoyo?
►
We are at school.
우리는 학교에 있어요.
ulineun haggyoe iss-eoyo.
►
We are having class / a lesson.
우리는 수업하고 있어요.
ulineun sueobhago iss-eoyo.
►




Those are the school children.
저들은 학생들이에요.
jeodeul-eun hagsaengdeul-ieyo.
►
That is the teacher.
저분은 선생님이에요.
jeobun-eun seonsaengnim-ieyo.
►
That is the class.
저건 반이에요.
jeogeon ban-ieyo.
►




What are we doing?
우리는 뭐하고 있어요?
ulineun mwohago iss-eoyo?
►
We are learning.
우리는 배우고 있어요.
ulineun baeugo iss-eoyo.
►
We are learning a language.
우리는 언어를 배우고 있어요.
ulineun eon-eoleul baeugo iss-eoyo.
►




I learn English.
저는 영어를 배워요.
jeoneun yeong-eoleul baewoyo.
►
You learn Spanish.
당신은 스페인어를 배워요.
dangsin-eun seupein-eoleul baewoyo.
►
He learns German.
그는 독어를 배워요.
geuneun dog-eoleul baewoyo.
►




We learn French.
우리는 불어를 배워요.
ulineun bul-eoleul baewoyo.
►
You all learn Italian.
당신들은 모두 이태리어를 배워요.
dangsindeul-eun modu itaelieoleul baewoyo.
►
They learn Russian.
그들은 러시아어를 배워요.
geudeul-eun leosia-eoleul baewoyo.
►




Learning languages is interesting.
언어를 배우는 것은 흥미로워요.
eon-eoleul baeuneun geos-eun heungmilowoyo.
►
We want to understand people.
우리는 사람들을 이해하고 싶어요.
ulineun salamdeul-eul ihaehago sip-eoyo.
►
We want to speak with people.
우리는 사람들과 말하고 싶어요.
ulineun salamdeulgwa malhago sip-eoyo.
