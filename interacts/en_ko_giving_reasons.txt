Why aren’t you coming?
당신은 왜 안 와요?
dangsin-eun wae an wayo?
►
The weather is so bad.
날씨가 너무 나빠요.
nalssiga neomu nappayo.
►
I am not coming because the weather is so bad.
저는 날씨가 너무 나빠서 안 가요.
jeoneun nalssiga neomu nappaseo an gayo.
►




Why isn’t he coming?
그는 왜 안 와요?
geuneun wae an wayo?
►
He isn’t invited.
그는 초대 받지 않았어요.
geuneun chodae badji anh-ass-eoyo.
►
He isn’t coming because he isn’t invited.
그는 초대 받지 않아서 안 와요.
geuneun chodae badji anh-aseo an wayo.
►




Why aren’t you coming?
당신은 왜 안 와요?
dangsin-eun wae an wayo?
►
I have no time.
저는 시간이 없어요.
jeoneun sigan-i eobs-eoyo.
►
I am not coming because I have no time.
저는 시간이 없어서 안 가요.
jeoneun sigan-i eobs-eoseo an gayo.
►




Why don’t you stay?
당신은 왜 안 머물러요?
dangsin-eun wae an meomulleoyo?
►
I still have to work.
저는 아직 일해야 해요.
jeoneun ajig ilhaeya haeyo.
►
I am not staying because I still have to work.
저는 아직 일해야 해서 머무르지 않아요.
jeoneun ajig ilhaeya haeseo meomuleuji anh-ayo.
►




Why are you going already?
당신은 왜 벌써 가세요?
dangsin-eun wae beolsseo gaseyo?
►
I am tired.
저는 피곤해요.
jeoneun pigonhaeyo.
►
I’m going because I’m tired.
저는 피곤해서 가요.
jeoneun pigonhaeseo gayo.
►




Why are you going already?
당신은 왜 벌써 가세요?
dangsin-eun wae beolsseo gaseyo?
►
It is already late.
벌써 늦었어요.
beolsseo neuj-eoss-eoyo.
►
I’m going because it is already late.
저는 벌써 늦어서 가요.
jeoneun beolsseo neuj-eoseo gayo.
►
