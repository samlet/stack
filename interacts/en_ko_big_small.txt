big and small
커요 그리고 작아요
keoyo geuligo jag-ayo
►
The elephant is big.
코끼리는 커요.
kokkilineun keoyo.
►
The mouse is small.
쥐는 작아요.
jwineun jag-ayo.
►




dark and bright
어두워요 그리고 밝아요
eoduwoyo geuligo balg-ayo
►
The night is dark.
밤은 어두워요.
bam-eun eoduwoyo.
►
The day is bright.
낮은 밝아요.
naj-eun balg-ayo.
►




old and young
늙었어요 그리고 젊었어요
neulg-eoss-eoyo geuligo jeolm-eoss-eoyo
►
Our grandfather is very old.
우리의 할아버지는 매우 늙었어요.
uliui hal-abeojineun maeu neulg-eoss-eoyo.
►
70 years ago he was still young.
칠십 년 전에 그는 아직 젊었어요.
chilsib nyeon jeon-e geuneun ajig jeolm-eoss-eoyo.
►




beautiful and ugly
아름다워요 그리고 못생겼어요
aleumdawoyo geuligo mos-saeng-gyeoss-eoyo
►
The butterfly is beautiful.
나비는 아름다워요.
nabineun aleumdawoyo.
►
The spider is ugly.
거미는 못생겼어요.
geomineun mos-saeng-gyeoss-eoyo.
►




fat and thin
뚱뚱해요 그리고 말랐어요
ttungttunghaeyo geuligo mallass-eoyo
►
A woman who weighs a hundred kilos is fat.
백 킬로가 나가는 여자는 뚱뚱해요.
baeg killoga naganeun yeojaneun ttungttunghaeyo.
►
A man who weighs fifty kilos is thin.
오십 킬로가 나가는 남자는 말랐어요.
osib killoga naganeun namjaneun mallass-eoyo.
►




expensive and cheap
비싸요 그리고 싸요
bissayo geuligo ssayo
►
The car is expensive.
자동차는 비싸요.
jadongchaneun bissayo.
►
The newspaper is cheap.
신문은 싸요.
sinmun-eun ssayo.
